Electronics & Computer Engineering Technology Access

Certificate in Electronics & Computer Engineering Technology Access

Get a head start in Electronics & Computer Engineering

The fast-growing world of electronics and computer technology

Missing admission requirements for the Electronics & Computer Engineering Technology - Renewable Energy program? Don't give up - this Access program opens the door to this popular Camosun tech-career program. Upgrade your skills in math, English and physics while you learn about computers, computer-aided design, electronics and test equipment. Successful completion gives you a guaranteed seat in the next intake of the program.

Admission requirements

Effective Date: September 2016

When you apply you will need to submit proof that you meet the following admission requirements.

Tip Where a letter grade is specified, you must have proof of that grade or higher.

Requirement High school course Camosun course Alternatives
English
one of:
  • C in English 10
  • Non-BC high school
  • C in ENGL 050
  • Camosun assessment
  • External assessment
Math
one of:
  • B in Principles of Math 10
  • B in Foundations of Math and Pre-calculus 10
  • C in Principles of Math 11
  • C in Pre-calculus 11
  • Non-BC high school
  • B in MATH 053
  • B in MATH 072
  • C in MATH 073
  • B in MATH 135
  • C in MATH 137
  • Camosun assessment

Note: It is highly recommended that the academic admission requirements be completed within the past five years. If more than five years has elapsed, upgrading in the required subjects is recommended.

Completion requirements

  • Students must complete all courses and achieve a minimum GPA of at least 2 to graduate from the program.
  • Students must also obtain a COM in LRNS 102 and a C or higher in ENGL 130, MATH 139, MATH 107, and PHYS 101.
